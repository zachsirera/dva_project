This dashboard presents an analysis of all of Donald Trump's tweets from his account's inception to today.

To accomplish this tweets were parsed and classified using a trained Naive-Bayes classifier to assign subjet lables
and then fed through semantic analysis algorithms to determine tweet sentiment, subjetivity, and complexity.

This dashboard aims to illuminate trends in President Trump's bahavior based on the contents of his tweets,
particularly as they are relevant to specific events and topics.
